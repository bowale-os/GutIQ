# Data Sources

GutIQ relies on licensed, open medical datasets.

**Primary Open Sources**
- **NHS Health Information (Open Government Licence v3.0)** – Used for plain‑language medical context.
- **PubMed Central (Open Access Subset)** – Used for technical literature summaries.
- **ClinicalTrials.gov summaries** – To map symptom‑treatment correlations.

**Data Licensing**
- NHS content © Crown copyright, used under the Open Government Licence.
- PubMed Open Access Subset contains public domain or CC BY content.

**Ethical Note**
Only publicly available, research‑friendly texts are ingested for semantic analysis. No private health data or copyrighted clinical text is used.
